# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, creative directors, and design leads at Italian studios and European startups. Viewed on desktop during candidate review; occasionally on mobile at events. They are assessing: Does she understand people? Can she design clearly? Is she distinctive?

## Product Purpose

Portfolio of Caterina Maria Cozzoli, Junior UX/UI Designer. Exists to convert a curious visitor into someone who reaches out. Success = a message sent or a CV downloaded.

## Brand Personality

Cura · Precisione · Calore. Empathetic but rigorous. Brazilian warmth meets Milanese precision. Inclusive by conviction, not compliance. She listens before she designs.

## Anti-references

- Generic "AI portfolio" cream/sand backgrounds with purple accents
- Overcrowded project grids with identical cards
- Playful/bubbly aesthetics that undermine credibility
- Cold, purely minimal all-white portfolios that erase personality
- Anything that looks like a Webflow template

## Design Principles

1. **Pratica ciò che predica** — accessibility-first; WCAG AA minimum on all text
2. **L'identità è nello spazio** — generous negative space lets the name and work breathe; resist filling every corner
3. **Calore senza dolcezza** — warmth through material choices (terracotta, weight, texture) not saccharine color
4. **Le origini come forza** — Milano and Fortaleza are not decoration; they are the brand
5. **Mostrare, non dichiarare** — let projects and micro-interactions prove the designer's skill

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. All images have meaningful alt text. Color is never the only signal. Language toggle for IT/EN.
